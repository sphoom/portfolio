Detailed Analysis

Project Overview

This interactive sales dashboard was built in Power BI to provide comprehensive visibility into sales performance, SPL (Sales Per Line Item), and customer/product insights. It dynamically updates based on the selected year, enabling year-over-year comparisons across gross sales and efficiency metrics.

A key feature is the integration of a currency switch using calculated DAX measures and slicers. Users can toggle between THB and USD without duplicating visuals, suitable for gloabal companies. The dashboard also highlights top and low-performing customers, product lines, and time-based trends.

Data Preparation & Modeling

  • Cleaned data: the raw data includes several error such as different date format mixed together, Null value, duplicates,etc.
  • Created custom DAX measures for currency switching and time intelligence (YoY, QoQ, MoM).
  • Implemented row-level filters and slicers for flexible analysis across product's applicatons, platforms, and time.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Yearly Comparison: Automatically compares selected year against previous year across all metrics.
  • Currency Toggle: Supports both THB and USD, using conditional DAX logic tied to slicer values.
  • Sales and SPL Breakdown: Monthly and quarterly visualizations with dual-axis to track gross sales and average SPL together.
  • Top Customers & Product Lines: Identifies high-contributing entities for focused business development.
  • Low SPL Analysis: Highlights underperforming products and customers to flag for cost efficiency reviews.
  • Fighting Sheet Tracker: Special monthly performance tracking for promotional or targeted sales efforts.

Technical Implementation

  • Custom DAX for dynamic metrics and KPI tracking.
  • Power Query transformations for data loading and cleaning.
  • Support for large datasets using incremental refresh patterns.

Conclusion

This dashboard allows users: sales team, finance team, and operation team, to monitor YoY performance as well as realtime performance. Enable them to make decisions regarding product inventory, pricing startegy, and customers focus.
